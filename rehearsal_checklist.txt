Rehearsal Checklist for Award Event:

1. Confirm availability of all key participants (speakers, presenters, technical staff).
2. Test audio-visual equipment (microphones, projectors, screens).
3. Run through the welcome speech and opening remarks.
4. Practice award presentations and transitions.
5. Test entertainment segment (if applicable).
6. Review closing remarks and thank you notes.
7. Address any technical or logistical issues.
8. Gather feedback from participants and make necessary adjustments.
